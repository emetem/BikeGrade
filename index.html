BikeGrade

Mobile

BikeGrade shows road incline (gradient) in real time using your phone’s orientation sensors—so you can see grade while you ride, with optional overlay and local recordings. The app is designed offline-first: no account required, and no analytics SDKs in the app.

  • Live incline display (degrees or percent), calibration, and graphs
  • Optional overlay / bubble on supported Android setups for glanceable grade
  • Local recordings of sampled incline over time; settings stay on your device
  • Published on app stores under developer name emetem.com

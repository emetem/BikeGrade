Privacy Policy — BikeGrade

Last updated: 28 March 2026

1. Controller / publisher

The application BikeGrade is published and distributed by emetem.com (website: https://emetem.com).

  • Legal status: The publisher is a natural person (sole developer), not a registered company. A future trade name emetem may be used if a legal entity is formed later; these documents will be updated if that happens.
  • Publisher / developer name (store listing): emetem.com
  • Contact (support & privacy): emetemdev@gmail.com — use the same address in the Google Play / App Store listing and on this site.

2. What data the app processes

BikeGrade is designed to work offline on your device. The app does not require you to create an account and does not send your recordings or settings to any server operated by the publisher.

The app processes the following categories of data:

  1. Device orientation and incline data — Used to compute the current incline/grade and to display it in the overlay and in graphs. Values come from on-device sensors and are kept in memory and, if you enable recording, in local storage.
  2. Local recordings and settings — When you record, the app stores sampled incline values over time with timestamps. Settings (units, theme, thresholds, overlay position and size) are stored locally. Data is stored only on your device using encrypted storage where supported, or private app storage otherwise. Recordings are limited in size and count.
  3. Diagnostics and error messages — The app may show local warnings (for example when a storage limit is reached). It does not automatically send diagnostics to the publisher.

3. Data that is not collected

BikeGrade does not:

  • collect your name, email, or contact details through the app itself;
  • collect account identifiers or login information;
  • collect or share your precise GPS location;
  • upload your recordings or settings to servers controlled by emetem.com;
  • include third-party analytics (e.g. Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics) or advertising SDKs;
  • show ads or use advertising identifiers.

4. Legal basis and purposes

Processing is performed solely to provide the app’s functionality: displaying incline, overlays, recordings on your device, and remembering preferences. Because data stays on your device, the basis is your choice to use the app and, where applicable, legitimate interests in operating the app and meeting store requirements.

5. Data sharing and transfers

The publisher does not receive your personal data from the app by default and does not share it with third parties through the app. No data is intentionally transferred off your device by BikeGrade.

If you manually export or share screenshots or content, that is governed by the tools you use.

Google Play and your device manufacturer may process limited technical or account data under their own policies.

6. Retention and deletion

  • Recordings and settings remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.
  • You can delete individual recordings inside the app.
  • Uninstalling removes app data as part of the normal Android uninstall process.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location (e.g. EEA/UK), you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing, and to complain to a supervisory authority. Because there is no backend receiving your recordings, your main controls are to manage data in the app or uninstall.

Questions: emetemdev@gmail.com

9. International users

If you use the app outside Slovakia, local laws may also apply. The publisher is identified as emetem.com for store and legal purposes.
